Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering

Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering focuses on the design, development, and deployment of systems that transmit and receive information. It plays a vital role in areas like communication networks, embedded systems, VLSI design, signal processing, and wireless technologies.

From antennas and RF systems to IoT and 5G networks, this discipline combines hardware design, mathematical modeling, and system-level optimization to build the connected world.

In this section, you'll find technical notes and practical insights related to modern telecommunication systems.
